Drawing

When you open the program the drawing line tool will already be selected for you to start drawing your deck. Drawing lines and inserting posts will snap to the grid. Posts lines and measurements will "half-snap" which means they can snap anywhere half of the width of a box. To turn off snapping you can click the grid icon in the middle left of the top bar. Insert posts into your drawing by right clicking and the post will be placed where your cursor is. If you want to delete a post hover over that post with your cursor and click the "Delete" button. on the top right of the icon bar will be the post types. If you are estimating glass or picket railing you will only need to use undrilled posts, if you are doing cable railing you will need to use the otehr posts. if you hover over the icon with your cursor it will tell you what type of post that is. You can select posts by clicking the icon or using the mouse scroll wheel for quick post changing.
