Welcome to our residential estimating program help guide! This tool has been used for years my PRO's very own staff and now we have opened it up to our contactors to use too! Quick Start Guide

After opening the program you will have a blank grid for drawing, to the left you will have an item list that can be opened or closed by clicking the gray arrow. To the right is the rail section calculator where it will show you your deck runs and it will show you how much top rail scrap when assembling your railing. At the top is a large bar with all your tools. The tools are divided into sections by "|". Going from left to right you have your file tools, then your calculating tools, then drawing scale, top railing selector, color, post selector, program version and finally the help button!
